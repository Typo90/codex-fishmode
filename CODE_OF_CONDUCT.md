# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Respect different experience levels.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Assume good intent, and clarify when unsure.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruption of project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.
